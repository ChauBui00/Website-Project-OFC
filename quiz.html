The beautiful hair color you wear should complement your skin tone, so take this quiz to discover your ideal shade

1. What color veins do you have?

  • Blue or purple veins = cool skin
  • Green or olive veins = warm
  • Blue or green veins = neutral

2. Jewelry test : what type of jewelry look best on you?

  • Silver Jewelry = Cool
  • Gold Jewelry = Warm

3. Mirrow test : place a white sheet of paper besides your face, and compare it to the mirrow.

  • Yellow = Warm
  • Pink/Rosy/Bluish-Red = Cool
  • Both = Neutral

4. Sun test : Do you tan easily?

  • Yes, I tan easily = Warm
  • No, I burn and turn red = Cool

5. Eye color test: What color is your eyes?

  • Blue/grey/green/deep brown = Cool
  • Brown/hazel/honey = Warm
  • Neutral : mixture of all kinds = Can be any color.
